Commercial Property & Casualty Insurance
Risk Intelligence, Underwriting Systems, and Insurance Digitalization

About

I work at the intersection of commercial property and casualty insurance practice and data-driven underwriting systems. My work focuses on structural inefficiencies in document-centric insurance workflows, risk information governance, and insurance market digitalization.
